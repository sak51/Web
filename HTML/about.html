ABOUT US


Welcome to Platinum Pavilion , your one-stop solution for all your hotel booking needs.
Who We Are
Founded in 2015, Platinum Pavilion is a premier online hotel booking platform dedicated to making hotel booking a seamless and enjoyable experience. Our team comprises of travel enthusiasts and technology geeks who are committed to bringing you the best hotel deals from around the world.
What We Do?
At Platinum Pavilion, we connect travelers with the world’s largest selection of incredible places to stay – including everything from apartments, vacation homes, to 5-star luxury resorts.
Our Mission
Our mission is to empower people to experience the world. We invest in digital technology that helps take the friction out of travel. At Platinum Pavilion, we connect millions of travelers with memorable stays and experiences, so they can get out and get more out of life.
Join us in our journey to make hotel booking a hassle-free experience. Happy Travelling!
